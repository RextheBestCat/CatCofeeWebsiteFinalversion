US

At CoffeeCats, we blend the best of both worlds: aromatic specialty coffee and cozy cats to make your visit unforgettable. Our passion is serving exceptional coffee while providing a warm and relaxing space where you can unwind, sip your favorite brew, and enjoy the company of our furry friends.
We stand for equality and tolerance. For each coffee we give away 10 cents to charity.

Our Story

CoffeeCats was founded with a simple mission: to create a haven where coffee lovers and cat enthusiasts could gather, relax, and connect. Our founder, a long-time coffee aficionado and cat lover, envisioned a place where you could have your coffee and pet a cat at the same time. After months of planning, our cozy cafe became a reality – a unique fusion of rich espresso and delightful feline energy.

Our Coffee

At CoffeeCats, we take pride in our specialty coffee. Each cup is made with the finest beans, carefully sourced from local roasters who share our passion for quality. Whether you’re in the mood for a smooth latte, a bold espresso, or a fun coffee-based creation, we’ve got something for everyone.
